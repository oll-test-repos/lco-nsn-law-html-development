Lac Courte Oreilles Tribal Code of Law.

1.401 Duties Of The Tribal Governing Board

(1) The Tribal Governing Board shall:

(a) Oversee, supervise, and govern all aspects of tribal gaming;

(b) Effectuate policy associated with tribal gaming;

(c) Delegate authority as appropriate to the Executive Director, tribal officers and other instrumentalities of the tribal government;

(d) Promulgate rules relating to the issuance, renewal, amendment, suspension and revocation of gaming licenses and the conduct of such activity upon the Reservation;

(e) Receive recommendations from the Executive Director to approve, license, and suspend or deny tribal licenses as to gaming activities;

(f) Establish terms and conditions as to the distribution of lottery tickets associated with a tribal lottery;

(g) Appoint the Chief Executive Officer and the Gaming Commission Executive Director;

(h) Determine the powers and duties of the Gaming Commission and the Board of Gaming Commissioners in addition to those set forth under this ordinance;

(i) Designate areas on the Reservation on which the selling of gaming supplies and equipment may be purchased and sold by approved suppliers to licensees;

(j) Delegate oversight to the Executive Director as to the fitness of all contractors providing supplies and equipment through contracts exceeding ten thousand dollars ($10,000.00) in value.

(2) The Tribal Governing Board, by formal action, shall appoint a Liaison(s) which shall be appointed to facilitate communication and coordination between the Tribal Governing Board and the Board of Gaming Commissioners. The Tribal Governing Board Liaison(s) shall not be considered a member of the Board.

(3) No member of the Board of Gaming Commissioners, nor tribal official, nor Executive Director shall participate in the approval, denial, revocation, or suspension of any application or license of an organization of which he or she is an officer, director, shareholder, member, or if any member of his or her immediate family, or in any matter affecting the scope of business.
