Lac Courte Oreilles Tribal Code of Law.

7.603 Grounds for Evictions

A tenant may be evicted for:

(1) Nonpayment of rent under a lease agreement when such payments are not made after ten (10) calendar days of the agreement date of payment, or ten (10) calendar days following the first day of the month in a month-to-month tenancy.

(2) Any agreement in rent, costs, or damages which have been due and owing for thirty (30) calendar days or more. The receipt by the Housing Authority of partial payments under an agreement shall not excuse the payment of any balance due upon demand.

(3) Nuisance, intentional or reckless damage, destruction, or injury to the property of the Housing Authority or other tenants or disturbing another tenant's right to quiet enjoyment of a dwelling unit.

(4) Serious or repeated violations of the rental agreement, any reasonable rules or regulations adopted in accordance with Subchapter 7.5 of this ordinance, or any applicable building or housing codes pursuant to ยง 7.903 of this ordinance.

(5) Occupation of any dwelling without permission or agreement, following any reasonable demand by a person in authority over the dwelling to leave.

(6) Under other terms in the rental agreement which do not conflict with the provisions of this Code.
