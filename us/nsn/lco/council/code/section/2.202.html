Lac Courte Oreilles Tribal Code of Law.

2.202 General Acronyms

Any acronym not defined in this Section shall be given its ordinary meaning. The following acronyms, wherever used in this ordinance, shall be construed to apply as follows, except where the context indicates otherwise:

(1) A/E – Architect/Engineer

(2) ABA – American Bar Association

(3) CFR – Code of Federal Regulations

(4) CPO – Chief Procurement Officer

(5) CSS – Community Supportive Services

(6) DOE – Department of Energy

(7) DOL – Department of Labor

(8) HUD – U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development

(9) ICE – Independent Cost Estimate

(10) HIS – Indian Health Services

(11) LCO – Lac Courte Oreilles Band of Chippewa Indians

(12) LS – Legal Services

(13) MBDC – Minority Business Development Center

(14) MOC – Minority Owned Corporation

(15) PHA – Public Housing Agency

(16) Pub. L. – Public Law

(17) RFP – Request for Proposals

(18) SBDC – Small Business Development Center

(19) TGB – Tribal Governing Board

(20) U.S.C. – United States Code

(21) WBC – Women's Business Centers

(22) WBE – Woman's Business Enterprise
