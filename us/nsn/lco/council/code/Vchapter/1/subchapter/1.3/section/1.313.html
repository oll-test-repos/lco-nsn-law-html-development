Lac Courte Oreilles Tribal Code of Law.

1.313 Ballot Order

(1) The names of all nominees shall be drawn from a "hat" and placed in that order on the official ballot, provided:

(a) The Nominee has signed the "Acceptance Form" within the ten (10) working days allowed pursuant to § 1.311 of this ordinance;

(b) The Nominee has completed the "Prerequisite Certification" within the ten (10) working days allowed pursuant to § 1.311 of this ordinance; and

(c) The Nominee has received written notification from the Election Committee that the s/he has satisfied the prerequisites established in § 1.312 of this ordinance; or received an order from the Tribal Court overturning the Election Committee's determination that s/he has failed to meet the prerequisites established in § 1.312 of this ordinance.

(2) In the event that a nominee subsequently withdraws from the election, the nominees will be moved up in proper order.
